ABOUT US

In the southernmost part of the Indian subcontinent lies Tamil Nadu, bordering the states of Kerala and Karnataka is a sure reminder to visit the breath-taking views of natural greenery, dense wilderness rich in ﬂora and fauna, the historic land with seas, and freshwater rivers ﬂowing across the state making its land fertile for perfect agricultural vegetation as it favors good climatic conditions. This state splits into 38 districts and paves the path to indigenous spices, condiments, temperate-zone vegetables, aromatic essential oils, and cash crops. After various researches with the help of specialists, we along with our endearing team have chosen the land for our Company's Warehouse.
Directly procured from the well-known organic farmhouses of (TN) our premium quality of farm-fresh products is handpicked, naturally dried, and stored hygienically. Our climate-control spice is warehoused and hygienically processed to lock the natural aromas and ﬂavors of the products. Processed in hygienic premises, untouched by human hands and packed after several quality checks, Lymoon products are then sent to our outlets and distributed to wholesale dealers across the country.
